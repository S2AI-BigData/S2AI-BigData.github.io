Call For Papers

Our workshop encourages research submissions including (but not limited to) the following list of topics:

  • Access Control for AI Agents
  • AI Agents for automating infrastructure tasks (e.g., anomaly detection, incident remediation, supply chain)
  • AI Agents for malware detection
  • Benchmarking for AI Agents deployed in Big Data Infrastructure Systems
  • Compliance and Regulations for AI Agents
  • Explainability for AI Agents
  • Guardrails for AI Agents
  • Information Flow Control for AI Agents
  • Robustness for AI Agents (e.g., noise, fault tolerance, adversarial attacks)
  • Security Architectures for distributed AI Agents
  • Execution trace analysis for trustworthy AI Agents

Authors are invited to submit either of the following:

  • Full-length research papers up to 10 pages
  • Short papers up to 4 pages


Submission

Submissions to the workshop must be in PDF and should follow the IEEE BigData paper formatting and single-blind review policy (see link for more information). All accepted submissions will be included in the conference proceedings of IEEE BigData.
